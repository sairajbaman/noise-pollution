a word about us

Noise pollution is considered to be any unwanted or disturbing sound that affects the health and well-being of humans and other organisms.caused by noisy machines, loudspeakers, vehicles, and other objects producing sound at high decibels. Even rock concerts are a cause of noise pollution. Like air and water pollution, noise pollution also adversely affects our health.

A healthy lifestyle is only the secret of good helath.

Your music may be someone else's noise: keep the volume down, reduce sound pollution!

our services

Public Awareness and Education

we create educational materials and campaigns to raise public awareness about the impacts of noise pollution and the steps individuals can take to reduce their noise footprint.

Noise Complaint Managemen

we develop software or tools for efficiently managing and tracking noise complaints from the public, helping authorities respond to and address noise issues more effectively.
